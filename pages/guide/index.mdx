# it's mina

your server's guardian, mod tool, and vibe keeper. all in one.

### moderation tools

bans, kicks, purge commands, voice mod—everything you need to keep things under control. ngl, my multi-function purge is pretty clean.

### lossless music

play from youtube, spotify, soundcloud, apple music. apply filters. queue management. it's a whole vibe.

### economy system

bank commands, gambling, work, rob—your server's own economy. get rich or die trying. ◕‿◕

### web dashboard

manage settings from your browser. easier than typing commands. tbh, it's kinda slick.
