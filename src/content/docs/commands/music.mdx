## playback

### `/play`

play a song by name or URL.

| option | description | required |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| `query` | song name or url | yes |

**supported sources:** youtube, spotify, soundcloud, and more

**example:**
```
/play query:lofi hip hop
/play query:https://open.spotify.com/track/...
```

---

### `/search`

search youtube and pick from results.

| option | description | required |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| `query` | song to search | yes |

---

### `/pause`

pause the currently playing track.

---

### `/resume`

resume playback of a paused track.

---

### `/skip`

skip to the next track in the queue.

---

### `/stop`

stop playback and clear the queue.

---

## queue

### `/queue`

view the list of upcoming tracks.

| option | description | required |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| `page` | page number | no |

---

### `/shuffle`

randomize the order of tracks in the queue.

---

### `/loop`

loop the current track or queue.

| option | description | required |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| `type` | loop type: track, queue, or off | no |

---

## playback control

### `/seek`

jump to a specific timestamp.

| option | description | required |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| `time` | timestamp (e.g., "1:30", "90") | yes |

---

### `/volume`

adjust playback volume.

| option | description | required |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| `amount` | volume level (0-100) | no |

---

### `/np`

show the currently playing track with progress bar.

---

### `/lyric`

search for and display song lyrics.

| option | description | required |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| `query` | song name to search | yes |

---

## audio effects

### `/autoplay`

toggle automatic song recommendations when queue ends.

---

### `/bassboost`

adjust the bass level.

| option | description | required |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| `level` | bass level: high, medium, low, or off | yes |

---

## session

### `/leave`

disconnect the bot from voice channel.
