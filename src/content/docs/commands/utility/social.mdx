Heya, friendship fanatics! Ready to spread some love and good vibes? Use these super-fun social commands to show your pals how much they mean to you! (ﾉ◕ヮ◕)ﾉ*:･ﾟ✧

<Aside type="tip">
  🎉 Psst! All commands now use slash commands for extra pizzazz! Just type "/" and let the magic begin!
</Aside>

## Friendship Power-Ups

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="👀 Reputation Peek" icon="seti:eye">
    - `/reputation view [member]` - Sneak a peek at someone's coolness level! 
    
    Example: `/reputation view @CoolFriend`
  </Card>

  <Card title="🎁 Reputation Boost" icon="seti:gift">
    - `/reputation give [member]` - Sprinkle some star dust on your bestie! 
    
    Example: `/reputation give @AwesomePal`
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## How to Use

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="View Reputation">
    ```
    /reputation view @FriendName
    ```
    See how popular your friend is! Maybe they're secretly a celebrity? 🕵️‍♀️
  </TabItem>

  <TabItem label="Give Reputation">
    ```
    /reputation give @FriendName
    ```
    Boost your friend's cool factor! It's like giving them a virtual high-five! ✋
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

<Aside type="note">
  ⏰ Remember: You can give reputation once every 12 hours. Choose wisely, you social butterfly! 🦋
</Aside>
