## 🌸 Anime Reactions

Konnichiwa! Time to bring some anime magic to your server! Share cute reactions with your friends using my anime commands! (◕‿◕✿)

<Aside type="tip">
  💫 All reactions are now available through the `/anime` slash command! Just type `/anime` and pick your reaction!
</Aside>

## Usage Examples

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="Basic Usage">
    ```
    /anime hug @BestFriend
    ```
    Sends a cute anime hug gif with your friend! 🤗
  </TabItem>

  <TabItem label="Pro Tip">
    Chain different reactions to tell a story!
    ```
    /anime poke @Friend
    /anime hug @Friend
    ```
    First get their attention, then give them a big hug! 💕
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

<Aside type="note">
  ⏰ Remember: There's a 5-second cooldown between reactions to keep things fun and fair!
</Aside>
